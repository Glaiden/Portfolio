Systems Engineering Project 2

Automated voice recognition 2D food printer for education use

Project objective

Develop a food printer prototype as a proof of concept, designed to facilitate engaging, multi-sensory learning experiences during mealtime.

Click to download the full ProjectReport (PDF)

Outcome

The prototype is a automated 2D food printer system that uses the handheld Evebot printpen to print edible designs on to food, with audio spelling feedback and word display. System enhances customizability of the print design by enabling users to print out any word or short sentence they desire just by speaking through an external microphone connected to the system.

Systems Engineering Project 2

Tremor reduction handheld 2D food printer for people with Parkinson's

  • Sketch Credits: Mohamed Zidane Marican

    • Project objective

    Develop a food printer system to help food industries expand their customer base using formal systems engineering processes.

    Outcome

    The tremor reduction food printer system is a smart detection system that detects direction of the tremor and controls the onboard motors to counteract it, resulting in handle stability via auto correction. As part of the team that innovated this system.

    My Contributions

    As part of the team I researched the food industry that uses food printers and people with disabilities to gather information to help the team develop the prototype, examine and identify food printer's functionality to help the team understand more on our referenced system.

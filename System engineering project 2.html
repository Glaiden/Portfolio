Systems Engineering Project 2

Tremor reduction handheld 2D food printer for people with Parkinson's

  • Sketch Credits: Mohamed Zidane Marican

    • Project objective

    Develop a prototype of a food printer as a proof of concept, aiming to help food industries expand their customer base through the application of formal systems engineering processes.

    Outcome

    The tremor reduction food printer prototype is a smart detection system that detects direction of the tremor and controls the onboard motors to counteract it, resulting in handle stability via auto correction.

    My Contributions

    • Research food industry, gather problems faced and the demands of digital latte art.
    • Look into accessibility of people with disabilities into food industry businesses and their perspective.
    • Identify what a food printer can do
    • Form functionality based on needs of people with disabilities.
    • Analyse existing solutions to use as comparison or reference.

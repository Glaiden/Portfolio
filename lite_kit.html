Lite Kit

The objective of this project was to make a car with features to allow tracking of a certain colour at a safe distance. The car comprises of parallax propellor board, aluminium car frame, mechanum wheels, TCA9548A I2c bus, HC-SR04 I2c ultrasonic sensors, Roboclaw 2x15A Motor controllers, STM32, pixy2 camera and ICR18650 3.7v batteries

The car does not require a user's contant input it takes command from the algorithim in the STM32 to track the a board of a fixed colour signature. The pixy2 camera will read the colour signature and send a packet byte which will be read by the STM32. The STM32 will get the signature block size and calculate to find center point of the block, depending on the calculated value it will send the directional command to the parallax propellor board that is coded in spin to control motor movements to move the car to where the colour board is moved.

code for both STM32 and parallax propellor board will be in github
